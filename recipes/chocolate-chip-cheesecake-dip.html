Chocolate Chip Cheesecake Dip

Description

This dip is addicting! Imagine a chocolate chip cheesecake batter that is safe to eat. Served with graham crackers, this dip will quickly become a family favorite. Keep refrigerated until ready to serve.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup confectioners' sugar
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup miniature semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Beat cream cheese and butter together in a bowl until smooth.
  2. Add confectioners' sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla to the cheese mixture; stir.
  3. Stir chocolate chips through the cheese mixture.
